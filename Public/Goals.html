About

This page helps you set personal goals for you to accomplish. You can set Annual goals for long term goals, quarterly goals for goals every quarter, monthly goals for goals you want to accomplish within the month, weekly goals for goals within the week, and finally daily goals for goals within the day.

This helps you gain more motivation to do your tasks. If you know what you want to achieve, you will have motivation to do it. This is different from the planner because these goals are more personal and less required but they're still something you want to finish.
