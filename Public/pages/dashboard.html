About

How it works: Dashboard is your quick summary page. It automatically reads your tasks, goals, and habits from localStorage and shows key widgets like Tasks Left, Upcoming Deadlines, Progress Reminders, Habit Checklist, and Goal Progress.

How to use it: Check this page first to see what needs attention today, then jump to Planner to update tasks, Goals to adjust targets, or Notes for details. Use the habit checklist for daily consistency and review upcoming deadlines before starting work.
