About

How it works: Planner stores all tasks with due date, type, priority, progress, and optional goal link. Active tasks are sorted by due date, while completed tasks move to the archive so they still count in analytics.

How to use it: Add tasks from the top entry bar, assign realistic priority, and use Goal and Priority filters to focus your list. Update progress as you work, mark tasks complete when finished, and restore archived items if needed.
