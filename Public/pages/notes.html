About

How it works: Notes are stored as cards with a title, type, progress status, content, and color. The page saves everything in localStorage so your notes stay available between sessions on the same browser.

How to use it: Click + New Note, write your content in the rich editor, then set the note type and progress to keep cards organized. Use color coding for quick scanning, and update or delete cards as your tasks, reminders, and ideas change.
