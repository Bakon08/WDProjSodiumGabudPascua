About

This page is where you can put your personal notes and reminders. Each note becomes its own card that you can colour-code, tag with a type and progress status, and write rich formatted content inside.

Click + New Note to open the note editor. You can bold, italicise, and add lists to your content. Edit or delete any card at any time using the buttons on the card.
